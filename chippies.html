What happened to the Workbook Farm?


Look, the Workbook Farm was really cute. But since its launch, I have become quite taken with seagulls... how do they get to be everywhere? I was on a trip in Canberra, and there were seagulls. In Canberra. Famously, Canberra is nowhere near the sea.

I thought: what if this time I went weirder? What if the seagulls found their way online?

Or what if the seagulls had no meaning at all?
